History

Shimla, once the summer capital of British India, holds a rich history with colonial architecture and scenic charm. It remains one of India’s most loved hill stations.

Popular Places

The Ridge, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and Kufri are must-visit spots in Shimla. The breathtaking mountain views and cool weather attract tourists year-round.

Culture & Festivals

Shimla celebrates vibrant festivals like Summer Festival and Ice Skating Carnival, showcasing Himachali culture, music, and traditions.
